I'm Akhilesh P S! Nice to meet you.
I'm a Final year student pursuing B.Tech in Information Technology at SRM Institute of Science & Technology. With a strong foundation in web development and design, I specialize in creating intuitive and seamless user interfaces that solve real-world problems. My experience includes working as a UI/UX designer for various internships and projects, where I've honed my skills in tools like Figma, Framer, and Adobe XD, as well as in coding languages like ReactJS and NextJS.

I've been designing for over 2 years. I'm a T-shaped designer, inclining towards Interaction and Visual Design. I'm self taught, converting my insights to solutions via problem solving.

I love diving into design challenges, whether it's through wireframing, prototyping, or full-stack web development. My goal is to understand and solve the gap between functionality and user satisfaction, ensuring that every product I work on is not just visually appealing but also easy to use.
